2. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information from and about users of our Services, including:

2.1 Personal Data

Personal Data refers to information that can be used to identify you individually. We may collect the following types of Personal Data:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, postal address, phone number
  • Account Information: Username, password, account preferences
  • Financial Information: Wallet addresses, transaction history within our platform
  • Profile Information: Profile picture, biography, social media handles, and other information you choose to provide
  • Communication Data: Records of your communications with us, including support requests and feedback

2.2 Non-Personal Data

We also collect information that does not directly identify you but relates to your use of our Services:

  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type and version, time zone setting, browser plug-in types and versions, operating system and platform, device information
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our Services, including pages visited, time spent, click-through patterns, features used
  • Blockchain Data: Public blockchain addresses and transaction data visible on public blockchains
  • Aggregated Data: Statistical or demographic data that does not directly reveal your identity

Note on Blockchain Data: Due to the nature of blockchain technology, transactions on public blockchains are immutable and transparent. While we do not directly link blockchain addresses to your identity within our Services, third parties may be able to connect your blockchain activity to your identity.

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our Services and to hold certain information. Cookies are files with a small amount of data that may include an anonymous unique identifier.

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the functioning of our Services

  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our Services
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertisements and track marketing campaign performance

You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of our Services.

For more detailed information about the cookies we use, please see our Cookie Policy.
